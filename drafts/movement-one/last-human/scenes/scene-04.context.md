## Drafting Decisions

### Voice Choices

1. **Dream contamination strategy**: Allowed voice to shift inside dreams to take on textures of the experienced perspectives, while maintaining Last Human voice in framing passages
2. **Intensity over resemblance**: Recognition comes through FEELING the others' experiences, not seeing faces that look similar
3. **Fragment usage in transformation**: Increased fragments toward the end to convey dissolution and integration
4. **Silver-blue as Archive color**: Consistent visual texture distinguishing the Archive from the hostile ecology outside

### Structural Choices

1. **Five-part structure**: Opening (inside Archive) → Exploration (finding interface) → Archaeologist Dream → Algorithm Dream → Transformation/Waking
2. **Dream sequence transitions**: Minimal bridging - shift occurs through line breaks and register change
3. **Recursive observation**: The Algorithm experiencing the Archaeologist while the Last Human experiences the Algorithm - three-level recursion
4. **Integration not ending**: Dream doesn't "end" - it integrates; the experiences persist in waking awareness

### Philosophy Integration

1. **Intensity over Identity**: He experiences BEING them, not seeing people who look like him
2. **Eternal Return as simultaneous existence**: The dream is of NOW (in their respective nows), not past or future
3. **Pharmakon**: The dream dissolves aloneness but proves he was never alone - gift and wound together
4. **Augenblick**: The moment of vision that transforms - after this, simple solitude is impossible
