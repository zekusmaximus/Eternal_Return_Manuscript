# Context Document: m2-lh-01 "The Archive"

## Drafting Decisions

### Cold-Hands Transformation

The opening catches cold-hands from m2-algo-01 by presenting the cold as **connection across time**, not merely dying:

- Cold connects to "hands moving through blue-white data" (Archaeologist)
- Cold connects to "processing that experiences its own processing" (Algorithm)
- Cold becomes "the temperature of connection across time"

### Archive Exploration

The Archive's topology is conveyed through:

- Self-similar geometry (corridors within chambers within corridors)
- Deliberate vagueness about direction ("inward or outward")
- Crystals as living response system (humming, glowing with recognition)
- Avoidance of explicit technology terms (replaced "interface/terminal" with "light/surface")

### Protocol Fragment Discovery

First fragments encountered through:

- Crystalline surfaces that glow with symbols
- Understanding as apprehension rather than comprehension
- Algorithm dream-intrusion providing distributed perspective
- Tracing gesture activating the patterns

### Pharmakon Cost

Added explicit memory dissolution to show what the Last Human loses:

- Cannot remember what the sky looked like before
- Forgot his own name
- Understanding gained costs memories carried
- "The price of the cure is the dissolution of the one being cured"
