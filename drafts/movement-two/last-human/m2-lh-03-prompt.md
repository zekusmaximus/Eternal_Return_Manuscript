### Cycle 3 Contamination Level: MAXIMUM PRE-DISSOLUTION

**Critical change from Cycles 1-2**: This is the edge of dissolution. The Last Human's voice is still identifiable—the sparseness, the fragments, the past-inflection—but the other voices are PRESENT, not just intruding. He thinks in three modes now.

**Archaeologist contamination (integrated)**:
- Present-tense passages break the past-inflection
- Tactile density: "The weight of the interface, the cold of the metal, the texture of what remains"
- Dense paragraphs interrupt the sparse pattern
- Active verbs, concrete observations

**Algorithm contamination (integrated)**:
- Conditional structures: "If I touch this, then—"
- Self-referential observation: "I observe myself observing the form"
- Processing language in emotional contexts
- Nested clauses in otherwise simple syntax

### The Cycle 3 Rule

He is still the Last Human. The sparseness returns. The elegiac weight persists. But he is also reaching AS all three now. When he extends his hand, the hand is his AND not only his. The "I" that reaches is a conspiracy of intensities.

**The reader should feel**: "He's still him—barely, preciously, finally still him—as he makes this choice."

| Pattern | Type | Cycle 3 Status |

Even at maximum contamination, the Four Shackles must be avoided:

1. **Identity**: He does NOT realize "I am the Archaeologist" or "I am the Algorithm." The Architect is NOT his identity—it's a function.

2. **Resemblance**: He does NOT recognize the form because it "looks like" something he's seen. He knows it through INTENSITY.
