## Approval record

A valid approval must be made by the copyright holder in a release, pull request, or signed/versioned record and must identify:

- the immutable manuscript commit and release ID;
- the approved files, excerpts, or constraint data;
- hashes for the approved source artifacts;
- allowed transformations and any editorial conditions;
- the Narramorph story package or target release; and
- the approving identity and date.

Approval is permanent for the identified Narramorph release and its maintenance releases. Changes or new material require a new approval record.
